what is this?

Two rooms. One rule.

Buubo is for brains that get noisy. You dump everything into Things, no sorting, no priorities, no tags to overthink. It's just a bucket so your head is empty.

Then you pick ONE thing for Doing. Flow shows only that task with a timer. Mac menu bar, Android share, and web all sync to the same Doing. No AI planning, no backlog guilt, no chat.
